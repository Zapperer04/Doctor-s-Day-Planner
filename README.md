# Doctor's Day Planner

> **A Digital Platform for Doctors' Efficient Day Management**

## üìñ Overview
**Doctor's Day Planner** is a specialized web-based application designed specifically for medical professionals to organize their professional and academic calendars .

Unlike generic productivity tools, this platform caters to the multi-dimensional scheduling requirements of physicians . It unifies private practice sessions, hospital duty hours, surgery scheduling, and academic research activities into a single, cohesive portal .

The system features a **conflict-resolution engine** to prevent overlapping commitments and provides **adaptive reminders** prioritized by medical urgency .

---

## üåü Key Features

### 1. Conflict-Free Scheduling
* **Intelligent Resolution:** An engine that automatically detects and prevents overlaps between private practice, hospital shifts, and academic deadlines .
* **Unified View:** Manage private clinic appointments and hospital duties in one centralized dashboard .

### 2. Specialized Medical Task Management
* **Surgery Pre-Study:** Dedicated reminders and preparation task lists for upcoming surgeries to ensure readiness .
* **Patient History & Notes:** Maintain critical notes for patients to ensure preparedness for future visits .

### 3. Academic Integration (Unique Feature)
* **Research Management:** unlike standard hospital software, this planner supports deadline tracking for thesis submissions, journal papers, and conferences .
* **Prioritized Focus:** Helps balance the dual capacity of doctors as both practitioners and researchers .

### 4. Smart Notifications
* **Priority-Based Alerts:** Notifications are ranked by urgency (e.g., surgeries > research deadlines) and sent via Email or SMS .

### 5. Analytics & Dashboard
* **Visual Insights:** A central dashboard displaying tasks, calendar views, and productivity analytics .
* **Weekly/Monthly Planner:** Long-term views for better workload distribution .
